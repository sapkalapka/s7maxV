Egy dokkoló mindenhez

Az Empty Wash Fill Dock a Roborock eddigi legfejlettebb dokkolója,
amely még kényelmesebbé teszi az otthoni takarítást azok számára, akik a legtisztább padlót igénylik.

Önmosó

Egy egyedülálló mosórendszer tisztítja a mopot takarítás közben és a takarítás végeztével.

Önfeltöltő

Takarítás közben újratölti a porszívó víztartályát, akár 300 m2-es felmosási tartományig.

Öntisztító

Az öntisztító kialakításnak köszönhetően önmagát is megtisztítja a még könnyebb karbantartás érdekében.

Önürítő

Az automatikus portartály-ürítés akár 7 hétre tolhatja ki a dokkoló porzsákjának csereperiódusát.

Gyorstöltés

Töltsd fel 30%-kal gyorsabban, hogy hamarabb visszatérhessen a takarításhoz.

Felismeri és reagál

A gépi tanulással és a dedikált neurális feldolgozó egységgel működő S7 MaxV felismeri ezeket a veszélyeket és a látottak alapján eltérően reagál. Your Virtual Home

Recreate your home, along with all your furniture and floor types, right in the Roborock app. Automatically. During a clean S7 MaxV drops furniture it has recognized in position. Need to clean around the bed after a lazy breakfast? Just tap on your bed.
You can even view your home with a 3D map or navigate around it digitally with the Matrix map.

* App interface is subject to change without notice.

Save the worst for last

Using your furniture, S7 MaxV automatically recognizes bedrooms, living rooms, dining rooms, and bathrooms and will recommend an ideal cleaning order based on room type. That way, your bedrooms might get cleaned first, but your bathrooms will always be cleaned last. As it cleans, suction power and scrub intensity are automatically adjusted for maximum effectiveness in each room.

Privacy

It can’t be said enough that privacy and security are critical when you have a camera roaming your home. That is why for the purposes of obstacle avoidance, ReactiveAI 2.0 does not save a single image. No images saved means no images to lose. Not only that, S7 MaxV is certified as a safe, smart home product by TUV Rheinland and also meets ETSI EN 303 645 cyber security standards.

More Suction? Done and Dusted

The suction power has been cranked up. All the way up. From a maximum of 2500 Pa on the Roborock S6 MaxV all the way to 5100Pa on the S7 MaxV. That’s more than double the power. Fire up the Max+ mode, and all that power is yours.

Ready to Rise Up

Got carpets? Like freshly mopped floors? Perfect. Intelligent mop lifting means when a carpet is detected, the mop is lifted out of the way. Now you can get mopped floors and vacuumed carpets in one single clean.

Mop with the Power of Sound

Experience robot mopping like never before, with VibraRise sonic vibration technology that scrubs your floors up to 3,000 times per minute with consistently high pressure throughout the clean.

Upgraded Floating Brush

A durable all-rubber brush effectively resists hair tangles. It stays closer to even uneven floors through its multi-plain floating mechanism.

Check In for a Chat

Remote viewing has now been upgraded, so you can do more than just talk at the people at home. With an onboard mic, you can now talk with them no matter where you are.
